For students, all you need to do is register, it will ask you for details which you are required to fill

Some of the details one should look for them first at the "Check available courses" and "check institutions" at the homepage since some data require specific details for a more accurate result

Once you register, the system will come up with courses you are likely to get when you apply for the KUCCPS

For a more accurate result you need to consider the requirements bellow as we were not able to capture all the requirements since all istitutions have different creteria

The subjects required for Engineering are: One must get a minimum of A- overal results and Maths(A) and Physics(A-)

The subjects required for Medicine are: One must get a minimum of A overal results and Biology(A) and Chemistry(A)

The subjects required for Law are: One must get a minimum of A- History(A) and Cre(A)

The subjects required for Health science courses are: Biology(B-)

The subjects required for technology courses are: Maths(B-)
